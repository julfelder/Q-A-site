Washington University New Students Guide

Welcome To Wash U!

Welcome to Washington University In St. Louis! the goal of this website is to provide you with the information that you, as a new student, need to succeed. On the left, you can see the site navigation bar. Check out some of the different information we have to offer!

FAQ and Tips

The FAQ and Tips tab contains answers to frequently asked questions, aswell as invaluable tips from current students. Additionally, you can ask questions of our students directly if you can't find the answers you need.

Get Involved

This tab contains information about the different ways to get invoved in the Wash U community. From clubs, to student government, there is guaranteed to be something for everyone!

Stand Out

This tab describes the ways in which our Wash U community stands out from other schools! Visit this page to find out why you should be so proud to be a Wash U Bear!
